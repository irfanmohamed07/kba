# KBA MEN'S HOSTEL — COMPLETE KNOWLEDGE BASE

## GENERAL RULES
- Students must be inside the hostel premises by 9:00 PM.
- Silence hours are observed from 10:30 PM to 6:00 AM.
- Ragging is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate expulsion.
- Smoking, alcohol, and drug consumption are strictly banned.
- Use of electrical appliances like heaters or induction stoves in rooms is not allowed.
- Guests are not allowed inside hostel rooms without prior permission from the Warden.
- Students must carry their hostel ID card at all times within the campus.

## MESS TIMINGS & FOOD
- Breakfast: 7:30 AM – 9:00 AM
- Lunch: 12:30 PM – 2:00 PM
- Evening Tea/Snacks: 4:30 PM – 5:30 PM
- Dinner: 7:30 PM – 9:00 PM
- Special diet requests should be made to the Mess Committee at least a week in advance.
- Mess fee is included in the hostel fee and is non-refundable.
- Students can check the daily/weekly mess menu on the portal.

## GATE PASS RULES
- Gate passes must be applied for at least 24 hours in advance via the student portal.
- For emergency leaves, students must contact the Resident Tutor (RT) immediately.
- Outing permission is usually granted on weekends (Saturday afternoon to Sunday evening).
- Students returning after the permitted time must provide a valid reason signed by a parent.
- Gate passes can be tracked under "My Passes" section in the portal.
- Night-out passes require prior approval from the Warden and a parent's written consent.

## MAINTENANCE & CLEANING
- Maintenance issues should be reported through the 'Maintenance' section in the portal.
- Complaints are categorized: Electrical, Plumbing, Carpentry, Housekeeping, and General.
- Housekeeping requests for room cleaning can be made daily before 10:00 AM.
- Electrical work requests include fan repair, light replacement, socket issues, etc.
- Carpentry work includes furniture repair, door/window issues, etc.
- All maintenance requests can be tracked for status updates on the portal.

## MEDICAL FACILITIES
- A basic first-aid kit is available at the security desk 24/7.
- In case of illness, students should notify the RT or Warden immediately.
- A doctor visits the campus every Monday and Thursday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
- For emergencies, an ambulance service is available 24/7.
- Medical form must be filled on the portal for any health-related issues.

## ROOM ALLOTMENT & FACILITIES
- Room allotment is done by the hostel administration at the beginning of each academic year.
- Each room is furnished with a bed, study table, chair, and wardrobe.
- Students are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their rooms.
- Room changes are allowed only with the Warden's approval.

## WIFI & INTERNET
- High-speed WiFi is available across the hostel premises.
- WiFi issues should be reported under 'Maintenance' on the portal.
- Access is monitored; usage of the network for illegal activities is strictly prohibited.
